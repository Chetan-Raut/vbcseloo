About Us

Vidyabharti College, established in 2008 and located in the vibrant state of Maharashtra, has firmly established itself as a leading educational institution within the city. With a commitment to excellence in education, Vidyabharti College offers a comprehensive range of degree programs, including three undergraduate and one postgraduate course, all conducted in the full-time mode.

At Vidyabharti College, we take pride in being a premier institute for various academic disciplines. Our Master of Commerce (M.Com), Bachelor of Arts (B.A.), Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com), and Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) courses are designed to empower students with knowledge, skills, and experiences that will shape their future. We strive to provide a dynamic and nurturing environment where students can thrive academically and personally.

With a dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and a commitment to holistic education, Vidyabharti College stands as a beacon of learning in Maharashtra. Our goal is to not only impart academic knowledge but also to instill values, critical thinking, and a passion for lifelong learning. As we continue our journey of academic excellence, we invite you to join us and become a part of our rich educational legacy at Vidyabharti College.
